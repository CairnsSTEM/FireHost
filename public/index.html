Educators

A place to find educational resources, personal development opportunities and ideas.

Industry

A channel for demonstrating STEM related activies and finding events that compliment your business activities.

Makers

A great starting point for demonstrating Hacker talents, and promoting STEAM as a lifelong passion.

OBJECTIVES:

  • promote the delivery of STEM activities through collaborations between organstations;
  • work on growing science engagement;
  • promote the values of science, technology, engineering and mathematics
  • include schools, universities, Industry, research institutes, non-profit organisations, libraries, councils, government agencies, local businesses, and co-working spaces

Projects.

  • Share events, outcomes and regional STEM news here
  • Offer to talk about your STEM knowledge & experiences
  • Network special STEM activities
  • Find out what's happening STEM-wise in our region
  • Get connected & included
  • Hold STEM Fair

DIRECTION

To expand our hub of STEM resource sharing; lend awareness to local STEM issues and acheivements; audit and profile people and programs within our region; build opportunities for Teacher professional development sessions; supprot and promote a variety of STEM activites, (ie National Science Week) especially in our own Region (ie The Eco-Fair & our own STEM Fair).
